How long would you have to work in another country on minimum wage for a cup of cappuccino in a restaurant or a pair of Nike sneakers?
The world is inequal when it comes to the minimum wage, but just how inequal can be seen in our visualisation.
The product prices are from www.numbeo.com which is a crowd sourced site. Anybody can input how much a certain product costs in their city.
In March 2013 there were more than 80.000 users who shared product costs.
